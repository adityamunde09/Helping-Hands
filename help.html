Frequently Asked Questions

How do I request a service?

To request a service, simply fill out the service request form on our website and provide details about your requirements. Our team will get in touch with you shortly to discuss further.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept various payment methods, including credit/debit cards, PayPal, and bank transfers. You can choose the payment method that is most convenient for you.

How can I cancel or reschedule a service?

To cancel or reschedule a service, please contact our customer support team at least 24 hours before the scheduled appointment. They will assist you in making the necessary changes.
